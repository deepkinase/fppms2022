个人介绍

边阳阳，博士，西北大学生命科学学院教授，入选国家海外高层次人才青年项目（2021），德国洪堡学者（2019-2021）。博士就读于中国科学院大连化学物理研究所（2009-2015），师从德国慕尼黑工业大学Bernhard Kuster教授进行博士后研究（2018-2021）。致力于蛋白质组学分析新方法，博士后期间搭建了基于1.0 mm内径色谱柱的微升液相色谱串联质谱（mLC-MS/MS）系统，显著提高了蛋白质组学研究的分析通量、稳定性和质谱仪使用效率，该工作被评为ESI高被引论文，收录于Nat Commun杂志“2020 Top 50 Chemistry and Materials Sciences Articles”。发表SCI论文40余篇，包括第一作者发表在Nat Methods (1), Nat Chem Biol (1), Nat Commun (1), Anal Chem (3)等期刊，Google学术引用1800余次。


Yangyang Bian got his PhD degree from Dalian Institute of Chemical Physics, Chinese Academy of Sciences, his research focused on developing new methods to increase analysis depth of phosphoproteomics. He developed the phosphotyrosine peptide enrichment method based on SH2 superbinder, which is published in Nature Chemical Biology, 2016. He jioned Prof. Bernhard Kuster’s lab as a PostDoc (2018-2021), and was funded by the Alexander von Humboldt Foundation fellowship. He optimized the micro-flow LC-MS systems operating at 50 ul/min, demonstrating its super robustness, stability in routine proteomics analysis. This work is published in Nat Commun 2020, was selected as ESI highlight paper. He joined as a Professor in Northwest University of China in June 2021. His current research interest focuses on developing new tools and methods for post-translational proteomics analysis.

讲解摘要

作为纳升色谱串联质谱技术的一种替代技术，微升液相色谱串联质谱系统逐渐被应用于蛋白质组学研究。在过去2年多的时间内，通过38000个蛋白质组学样品的分析，我们证明了使用1 mm内径色谱柱和50 μL/min流速的微升液相色谱串联质谱系统具有极高的分析通量、稳定性和重现性。该系统可以每个月分析1500个样品，在色谱分离效率没有明显损失的情况下，单根色谱柱分析了超过14000个样品。我们测试了不同的样品类型和不同的数据采集方法，2小时的单针进样DDA和DIA分析都可以定量到7000种以上的蛋白质，证明了该微升液相色谱串联质谱系统在蛋白质组学研究领域具有极高的应用前景。


Micro-flow liquid chromatography tandem mass spectrometry (μLC–MS/MS) is becoming a viable alternative to nano-flow LC–MS/MS for the analysis of proteomes. We demonstrated the potential of such a system operating with a 1 mm i.d. × 150 mm column and at a flow rate of 50 μL/min for high-throughput, robust and reproducible proteomics on the basis of the analysis of ∼38,000 samples over the past two years. Up to 15,00 analyses were performed within one month, and >14,000 samples could be analyzed on a single column without loss of chromatographic performance. Different types of samples and acquisition methods were tested, more than 7,000 proteins could be quantified by either single shot DDA or DIA analysis with 2 hour gradient, demonstrating its great potential in proteomics research.
